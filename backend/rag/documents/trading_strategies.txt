# Comprehensive Trading Strategies Guide

## Overview
Trading strategies are systematic approaches to buying and selling financial instruments. Successful traders combine multiple strategies with proper risk management.

## Day Trading Strategies

### Scalping
- Timeframe: 1-5 minutes
- Target: Small profits (5-10 points on NIFTY)
- Stop Loss: 5-8 points
- Best for: High volatility sessions

### Momentum Trading
- Timeframe: 15-30 minutes  
- Entry: Breakout of consolidation
- Target: 1-2% move
- Stop Loss: Below recent low

### Mean Reversion
- Timeframe: 5-15 minutes
- Entry: Overbought/oversold levels
- Target: Return to mean
- Indicators: RSI, Bollinger Bands

## Swing Trading Strategies

### Breakout Trading
- Timeframe: Daily charts
- Entry: Break above resistance
- Target: Next resistance level
- Stop Loss: Below breakout point

### Pullback Trading
- Timeframe: Daily charts
- Entry: Pullback to support in uptrend
- Target: Previous high
- Stop Loss: Below support

## Position Trading Strategies

### Trend Following
- Timeframe: Weekly charts
- Entry: On trend confirmation
- Target: Trend exhaustion
- Stop Loss: 20% below entry

### Value Investing
- Timeframe: Long-term (6+ months)
- Entry: Fundamental undervaluation
- Target: Fair value
- Analysis: P/E, P/B, ROE ratios

## Indian Market Specific Strategies

### Bank NIFTY Trading
- Best time: 9:15 AM - 10:30 AM
- Key levels: Previous day high/low
- Volatility: Higher than NIFTY 50

### Index Options Writing
- Strategy: Sell OTM options
- Delta: 0.2-0.3
- Expiry: Weekly
- Risk: Unlimited (hedged with futures)

### Sector Rotation
- Auto: IT, Pharma, Banking
- Cycle: Quarterly results
- Entry: Sector momentum shift
- Risk: Concentration risk

## Risk Management Integration

### Position Sizing
- Day Trading: 1-2% of capital
- Swing Trading: 3-5% of capital  
- Position Trading: 5-10% of capital

### Stop Loss Rules
- Day Trading: 0.5-1%
- Swing Trading: 2-3%
- Position Trading: 15-20%

### Profit Taking
- Day Trading: 1:2 risk-reward
- Swing Trading: 1:3 risk-reward
- Position Trading: 1:5 risk-reward

## Market Timing Strategies

### Pre-Market Analysis
- Time: 8:45 AM - 9:15 AM
- SGX NIFTY correlation
- Global market cues
- Previous day close analysis

### Intraday Session Trading
- Morning Session: 9:15 AM - 11:30 AM
- Afternoon Session: 1:00 PM - 3:30 PM
- Avoid: 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM (lunch effect)

## Technical Analysis Integration

### Chart Patterns
- Head and Shoulders
- Double Top/Bottom
- Triangle patterns
- Flag and Pennant

### Indicator Combinations
- RSI + MACD for momentum
- Bollinger Bands + RSI for reversal
- Volume + Price for confirmation

## Algorithmic Trading Elements

### Entry Conditions
- Price action confirmation
- Volume spike validation
- Multiple timeframe alignment
- Risk-reward ratio > 1:2

### Exit Conditions
- Stop loss hit
- Target achieved
- Trend reversal signals
- Time-based exits

## Psychological Trading Rules

### Trading Discipline
- Follow the plan strictly
- No revenge trading
- Accept losses gracefully
- Review trades weekly

### Emotional Control
- Fear and greed management
- Overtrading avoidance
- Patience in waiting
- Confidence in analysis
